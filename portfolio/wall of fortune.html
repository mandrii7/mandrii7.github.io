Project Description

This project is an interactive art piece consisting of a wall

that has lots of small doors, each numbered from one to

twenty-four. Behind every door, there's a note with a wish

or call to action. Visitors start the activity by pressing

a button next to the wall. Once the button is pressed, the

wall will choose one of the doors to light up randomly.

Visitor then takes the note from the chosen door, keeps it,

and writes a new note on a piece of paper for the next

person to find.
