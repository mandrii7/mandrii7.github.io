Wall of Fortune

"Wall of Fortune" is the project that I and four other group members created during the Winter 2023 semester for the Physical Interfaces course in the Interaction Design program at George Brown College.

This project is an interactive art object, a wall with many small doors, each door numbered from one to twenty-four. Behind each door, there is a note with a wish or a call to do something. The visitor must press the button next to the wall to start the process. After the button press, the wall will randomly select one of the doors to start flashing. The visitor must pick up a note from the selected door, keep it, and use a pen and paper to write a new one for the next visitor.

"Wall of Fortune" was showcased at "YES!", the official grad show for George Brown College's School of Design. It received positive feedback for its ease of understanding, interactivity, unique concept, and recyclable design.
