ARoma

"ARoma" is the project on which I and three other group members worked on during Fall 2023 semester for the Immersive Media 1 course in the Interaction Design program at George Brown College.

This project is an idea of application called "ARoma". This application has an AR feature that provides a specific 3d food model by scanning a food menu. It also shows a dietary warnings, total price, and nutrition ingredients of the specific food that visitor scanned in menu. This idea was developed specifically for Markham Station restaurant, but we considered to make it more global in the future and develope it for any possible restaurant.

This idea received a positive feedback from Immersive Media 1 professor Steven Reaume for its clean design, high posibility of potential creation, detailed explanation of development process, and fulfillment of all requirements of assignment.
